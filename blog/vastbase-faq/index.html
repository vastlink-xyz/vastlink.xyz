Vastbase FAQ

Sep 5, 2025 · 2 min read

🙋‍♂️ What is Vastbase?

Vastbase is a next generation multisig wallet for digital asset management and onchain businesses, such as: Crypto treasuries (in BTC, ETH, SOL, stablecoins, ERC-20, NFT, …) , RWAs, DAOs and VC funds.
Vastbase is built on Lit Protocol, Lit Protocol is a key management network for decentralized signing and encryption.

🙋‍♂️ What problems is Vastbase solving?

Vastbase solves key barriers to mainstream onchain business adoption: unsafe wallet setups, unintuitive user experiences, and unsustainable pricing models.

🙋‍♂️ What is the solution?

● Bank-grade security with MPC (multi-party computation) and MFA (multi-factor authentication)
● Onboarding with email in 10 seconds
● Email notification driven workflows
● Cost-efficient “pay as you go” pricing model powered by tokenomics
● Unlimited features via the marketplace
● Open source and open infrastructures

🙋‍♂️ Why build Vastbase?

After the 1.4B Safe{Wallet} & Bybit hack in February 2025, we as a user of Safe{Wallet}, were looking for alternatives, and couldn’t find one, meanwhile a lot of people were looking for the same too.
Then we partnered with Lit Protocol to build Vastbase aiming to address the issues with current multisig wallet offerings.
It was relatively faster to build Vastbase, because we built it on top of Vastlink, a next generation wallet as a service, which is wallet protocol Vastlink built on Lit Protocol.

🙋‍♂️ Why partner with Lit Protocol?

Lit Protocol’s offerings make it possible for Vastbase to realize it’s vision.
● It’s MPC solution ensures security and removes single point of failure
● Lit’s programmable private key management solution provides a perfect tech stack for Vastbase to be built on
● Lit is OSS, decentralized, and community driven

🙋‍♂️ How Vastbase works?

1. Use email to sign up and sign in
2. Set up security as you go
3. Invite others to your teams via email
4. Raise and approve proposals by following email notifications
5. Send and receive crypto via email
6. Pay as you go, no subscription or upfront fees, no lock-in
